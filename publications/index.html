    Coarse-Grained Geometric Quantum Dynamics in the Tensor Network Representation
    Mo Sha and Bing Gu
    arXiv preprint arXiv:2601.16913, 2026
    Abs DOI Bib HTML

    Quantum geometrical molecular dynamics provides a quantum geometric picture for understanding reactive dynamics, especially excited-state conical intersection dynamics, and a numerically exact method for strongly correlated electron-nuclear dynamics. A coarse-grained local diabatic ansatz and tensor-network representations of the expansion coefficients and molecular time-evolution operator provide an accurate and computationally tractable approach to high-dimensional, fully quantum, strongly coupled electron-nuclear dynamics from first principles.
