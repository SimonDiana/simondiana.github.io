I am a Ph.D. student in Chemistry at Westlake University, advised by Prof. Bing Gu. My research focuses on theoretical and computational chemistry, particularly nonadiabatic quantum dynamics, local diabatic representations, quantum geometric molecular dynamics, and tensor-network methods for high-dimensional electron-nuclear dynamics.

I received my B.S. in Chemistry (Education) from Soochow University in 2024, graduating first in my class. My earlier research included visible-light-driven proton-coupled electron transfer and machine-learning force fields for phase-transition simulations.
